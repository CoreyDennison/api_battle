Spotify artist battle app.

This is a spotify artist battle app which works by entering two artist names and pressing the "Battle" button. Once that is done, the application will search the Spotify API for the two names and return with their name, popularity, number of songs, and their roles in the music industry, such as song writer, musician, singer, and so on. This information is then compared side by side, with the artist with the higher amount of an aspect receiving a point. These points are then tallied up, and the artist with the most points is the winner. Simples?
